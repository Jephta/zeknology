About Us

Zeknology is a technology oragnization. It provides various technologies such as Software, Training, Hardware, Content, etc. to the world. It also has a special focus on Intriguing mobile content where it collects, organizes, analysis, and delivers unique, local, and Intriguing content to the world.

Those who are interested in knowing and learning about less known content of the world will benefit from this app.

Currently, Zeknology, provides the following content to the world:

  • Training content. Training on mobile phones application (Mobile Apps) development.
  • Translation content. A translation app focusing on indigenous languages of the world.
  • Culture content. A tradition app focusing on various cultures of the world.
  • Music content. A music app focusing on various local and indigenous artists of the world
  • Family Tree content. A Family Tree app focusing on family trees of various families of the world showcasing their ancestors and descendants.
  • Autobiography content. An Autobiograohy app focusing on interested individuals who would like to record their own history and share it with the world.
